What is courage?

Is it quiet, is it loud,  
Is it humble, is it proud,  
Is it crying, maybe smiling?

Who are you?

Are you brave or are you scared,  
Are you judging, or forgiving,  
Are you happy, are you sad?

You are you, and this is courage.
