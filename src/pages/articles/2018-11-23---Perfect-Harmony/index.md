---

When I breath  
by your side  
in perfect  
harmony.  

Everything is  
alright.  

---

When you sing  
your lovely song  
with such  
joy.  

My heart  
weeps.  


---

I cannot believe  
the beauty of  
life.  

And the tragedy  
of  
death.
